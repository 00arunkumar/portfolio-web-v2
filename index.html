Who I Am

I’m a Data Analyst passionate about building intelligent data systems that improve reporting, decision-making, and business efficiency. With experience in analytics, ETL development, dashboard creation, and workflow automation, I work on converting raw and scattered data into structured, reliable, and insight-driven solutions.


My background includes working with Python, T-SQL, Power BI, Tableau, MySQL, and cloud technologies to analyze large datasets, design KPI-focused dashboards, and automate repetitive data tasks. I’m particularly interested in creating scalable analytics solutions that combine technical depth with real business impact.
